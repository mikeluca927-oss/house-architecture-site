# Services | House Architecture & Construction, PLLC

> Full-service residential design-build firm serving Westchester County, NY. All services delivered under one contract by licensed architect and general contractor Silvio M. Luca.

---

## Architecture

We provide complete architectural design services for residential projects in Westchester County. Services include schematic design, design development, construction documents, permit applications, and construction administration. As a licensed New York State architect, Silvio M. Luca stamps and files all drawings directly with local building departments — no outside architect required.

**URL:** https://www.housearchitectureandconstruction.com/architecture

## New Construction

We build fully custom homes on vacant lots throughout Westchester County. Our scope covers everything from site analysis and zoning review through architectural design, permit filing, complete construction, and certificate of occupancy. As a design-build firm, we manage every phase under one contract.

Towns served: Scarsdale, Eastchester, Bronxville, Larchmont, Pelham, Rye, Mamaroneck, and all Westchester communities.

**URL:** https://www.housearchitectureandconstruction.com/new-construction

## Historic Restorations

Preservation and restoration of Westchester County's historic homes. We have extensive experience with Tudor Revival, Colonial, Georgian, Craftsman, Shingle-style, and Victorian homes throughout Scarsdale, Bronxville, Larchmont, Pelham, and surrounding communities.

Our restorations preserve original architectural character — plaster detailing, period millwork, original windows — while upgrading all systems to current code and modernizing kitchens, bathrooms, and living areas.

**URL:** https://www.housearchitectureandconstruction.com/restorations
