## What We Do

House Architecture & Construction, PLLC is a design-build firm. That means architectural design and construction happen under one roof, managed by one person: Silvio M. Luca.

Most homeowners hire an architect and a contractor separately. The two firms miscommunicate, change orders multiply, and the client gets caught in the middle. We eliminate that entirely. Silvio designs the project and builds it. Same person, same standards, start to finish.

## Services

- **Residential Architecture** — Schematic design, construction documents, permit applications, and construction administration for new homes and renovations throughout Westchester County.
- **New Construction** — Custom ground-up homes on vacant lots. Site planning through certificate of occupancy.
- **Home Renovations** — Full-scale whole-home renovations, floor-by-floor transformations, and targeted room renovations.
- **Home Additions** — Second-story additions, primary suite additions, rear extensions, dormers, and attached garages.
- **Historic Restorations** — Preservation and restoration of Tudor, Colonial, Georgian, Craftsman, and Victorian homes throughout Westchester.
- **Kitchen Remodeling** — Custom layout, cabinetry, stone countertops, appliances, tile, and all MEP work.
- **Bathroom Renovations** — Primary suites, spa baths, guest baths, powder rooms — full scope from layout through finish.
- **Living Areas** — Open-concept conversions, custom millwork, built-ins, coffered ceilings, and finish coordination.

## Why Design-Build

When your architect and contractor are the same person:
- Designs are developed with real construction knowledge from day one
- No gap between what was drawn and what gets built
- No change orders from design-to-construction miscommunication
- Faster project delivery
- One contract, one point of accountability
