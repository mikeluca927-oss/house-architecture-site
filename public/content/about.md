## The Firm

House Architecture & Construction, PLLC delivers residential architecture and construction under one roof. We do not separate design from building. Silvio M. Luca designs your project, files the permits, and builds it. That single point of accountability is the foundation of everything we do.

Over 25 years, we have completed more than 99 residential projects throughout Westchester County — from full ground-up custom homes to targeted kitchen renovations in historic Colonials.

## Silvio M. Luca — Principal Architect & Builder

Silvio M. Luca is a Pratt Institute graduate who holds dual licensure in New York State as both a registered architect and a licensed general contractor. He personally oversees every project the firm takes on.

His approach is direct: understand what the client actually wants, design it with real construction knowledge, and build it to the same standard he would hold for his own home. Every project — regardless of scope — receives his personal attention from first consultation through final walkthrough.

## Our Process

**01 — Architecture**
Design begins with understanding your vision and how you live. Silvio develops the architectural concept, then full construction documents including all drawings required for municipal permit approval.

**02 — Estimate**
A detailed scope and estimate is developed once the design is set. You know exactly what you're getting and what it costs before construction begins.

**03 — Construction**
Silvio manages every trade on site — structural, mechanical, electrical, plumbing, carpentry, and finishes. No subcontractor coordination falls to you.

**04 — Finishing**
Every project is delivered with the same attention to detail in the finishing as in the structure. The difference between a good project and a great one is what happens at the end.
